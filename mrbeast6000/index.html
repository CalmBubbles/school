Jimmy Donaldson formerly known as MrBeast is an American YouTuber known for giving away money, crazy stunts and challenges. He made a channel called MrBeast6000(now MrBeast) and started making videos since he was 13(early 2012). On 2017, Jimmy went viral due to a video called Counting To 10000 In One Sitting which had a sequel called I Counted To 100,000!.
After that, he became very popular and most of his videos started gaining millions and millions of views. After a while, Jimmy started including challenges and giving away money in his content. He also started successful fundraisers with a former NASA engineer and YouTuber Mark Rober, which includes Team Trees. The goal of Team Trees is to raise $20 million which was surpassed and the fundraiser received over $23.89 million. In Team Trees they plant trees, $1 is equivalent to 1 tree. Which means they have planted over 23.89 million trees all over the world.
They also started another successful fundraiser, this time it's called Team Seas. In Team Seas the goal is to raise $30 million. This is similar to Team Trees. But the goal is to remove 30 million pounds of trash in the sea, $1 is equal to 1 pound. The fundraiser finished and raised over the goal, and they are removing trash as of today.
Jimmy also made another YouTube channel featuring helping people, charity and giving away essentials, called Beast Philanthropy. All of it's profit goes to charity.
